Major World Wars

Hundred Years' War

Leaders: Edward III, Henry V, Charles VII

Cities: Orléans, Paris

Weapons: Longbows, swords, early cannons

Summary: Dynastic struggle between England and France (1337–1453).

World War I

Leaders: Kaiser Wilhelm II, Woodrow Wilson

Cities: Sarajevo, Verdun

Weapons: Rifles, tanks, poison gas

Summary: Global conflict reshaping Europe (1914–1918).

World War II

Leaders: Churchill, Roosevelt, Hitler, Stalin

Cities: Berlin, Stalingrad, Hiroshima

Weapons: Tanks, aircraft, atomic bombs

Summary: Largest war in human history (1939–1945).

Historic Indian Wars

First Battle of Panipat (1526)

Leaders: Babur vs Ibrahim Lodi

City: Panipat

Weapons: Matchlock guns, artillery

Summary: Foundation of the Mughal Empire in India.

Battle of Haldighati (1576)

Leaders: Maharana Pratap vs Man Singh I

Location: Haldighati, Rajasthan

Weapons: Swords, spears, cavalry

Summary: Symbol of Rajput valor and resistance.

Battle of Plassey (1757)

Leaders: Siraj-ud-Daulah vs Robert Clive

City: Plassey

Weapons: Muskets, cannons

Summary: Marked the beginning of British dominance in India.
